About

A bit about us…

Dream Pop Bakery is based in Newcastle upon Tyne making beautiful handmade cake pops for any occasion. Beautiful little mouthfuls of cake on a stick, they need to be tasted to believe just how wonderfully yummy they are!

Cake pops can be designed in any colour / flavour / shape or pattern - just let your imagination run wild and we will do our best to meet your requirements. We can design and make plain and simple classic cake pops, right up to bespoke shaped or character cake pops - just let us know what you have in mind!

From children's parties to hen do's; weddings, birthday presents or special treats for that someone special - cake pops are loved by everyone young or old.
